What is Mystical Messages?

Mystical Messages is a subscription service for parents that makes childhood magic real. Send personalized SMS text messages to your child that appear to come directly from their favorite magical characters — Santa Claus, the Tooth Fairy, the Easter Bunny, and more.

Choose from dozens of pre-written message templates or write your own custom messages. Schedule them in advance for holidays, or send one instantly in an emergency moment — like when your child unexpectedly loses a tooth!

Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is Mystical Messages?
Mystical Messages is a web-based service that lets parents send SMS text messages to their children that appear to come from magical characters like Santa Claus, the Tooth Fairy, or the Easter Bunny. All messages are parent-controlled and fully customizable.
How are messages delivered?
Messages are delivered as standard SMS text messages to any US mobile phone number. The message appears to come from the magical character's number, making it feel real and special to your child.
Can I write my own messages?
Absolutely! While we provide 30+ pre-written templates for every occasion, you can always write completely custom messages in your own words using any character's voice.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings page. Your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period, and you will not be charged again after cancellation.
Is my payment information secure?
Yes. All payments are processed securely through Stripe, a leading payment processor trusted by millions of businesses worldwide. We never store your credit card details on our servers.
What is the $0.99 trial?
The $0.99 trial lets you send a single magical message to try the service before committing to a monthly subscription. It's a one-time charge with no automatic renewal.
