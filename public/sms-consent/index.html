SMS Consent & Opt-In

Mystical Texts LLC · mysticaltexts.com · hello@mysticaltexts.com

Mystical Messages is a service that lets parents send personalized SMS messages from beloved childhood characters — Santa Claus, the Tooth Fairy, and the Easter Bunny — directly to their own mobile phone. All messages are parent-initiated and delivered to the parent's phone number only. No messages are sent directly to children.

Who sends SMS messages

SMS messages are sent by Mystical Texts LLC operating as Mystical Messages, via Twilio's messaging platform. Messages are only sent to phone numbers explicitly provided and consented to by the account holder (always a parent or guardian aged 18+).

How users opt in

SMS consent is collected in two places during the account lifecycle:

1

The user creates an account at mysticaltexts.com using their email address and a password.

2

During the account setup step, the user enters their mobile phone number and must check the following opt-in checkbox before proceeding:

📱 In-app consent checkbox — required to complete setup

Users see the following checkbox during the phone number setup step. It is unchecked by default and must be checked before the account setup form can be submitted.

I agree to receive recurring automated text messages from Mystical Messages at the number provided. Msg & data rates may apply. Msg frequency varies. Reply STOP to cancel, HELP for help. Terms of Service · Privacy Policy

3

The user's consent is recorded in the database with a timestamp (sms_consent_at) at the moment they complete setup.

4

Existing users who signed up before the SMS consent requirement was introduced are shown a consent overlay the first time they log in after the policy went into effect. They must explicitly agree before any SMS messages can be sent to their number.

5

No SMS messages are sent until consent is recorded. Consent is never assumed.

Message types & frequency

  • Transactional messages initiated by the account holder (e.g., "Send a Santa message now")
  • Scheduled messages set up in advance by the account holder
  • One-tap "Oh-Crap!! Button" messages triggered manually by the account holder

Message frequency is entirely controlled by the account holder. Mystical Messages does not send unsolicited or marketing SMS messages to subscribers.

How to opt out

Users may opt out at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any SMS message — Twilio processes this immediately
  • Replying HELP to any SMS message for support contact information
  • Emailing hello@mysticaltexts.com to request removal
  • Canceling their account

Standard message and data rates may apply depending on the recipient's carrier plan.

Data & privacy

Phone numbers are used solely to deliver SMS messages from fictional characters that the account holder initiates. Numbers are never shared or sold. See our full Privacy Policy and Terms of Service for details.
